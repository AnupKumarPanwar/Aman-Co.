Description

The perfect dream home address designed to define the individuality of its patrons. Rosewood Estate is nestled in the serenity of the nature with maximum open spaces and stylishly landscaped lush green gardens.

The project covers an expanse of 25 acres with elegantly crafted villas, plots and independent floors. The unit sizes available starting from 980 sq. ft to 2700 sq. ft.

Well equipped with aesthetically appealing features such as uPVC windows, flush doors, vitrified flooring and oil bound distemper, along with dedicated facilities such as club, swimming pool, lush green gardens, and community centre.
